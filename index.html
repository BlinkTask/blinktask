About Me

Why Blink Task Exists

  • I know trusting a new website isn't easy – so I work harder on every single project to earn it.
  • I combine AI tools (ChatGPT, Canva, Cursor AI) with manual reviews to ensure every delivery feels unique and human.
  • I believe in fast turnaround, clear communication, and zero overpromises – you'll always know what to expect.

Why You Can Trust Me

  • New, but Fully Dedicated: I treat every task like it's my portfolio's showcase.
  • Transparent Process: I share drafts, templates, or samples before finalizing.

  • Clear Pricing: No hidden costs – flat, upfront pricing.

My Promise to Clients

  • I'll communicate openly about timelines and requirements.
